# Accessibility

- **Contrast:** Body/caption text over imagery should meet WCAG AA-ish contrast; use scrims behind text when needed.
- **Type size:** Captions on 1080p kiosks should generally stay at or above ~28 px CSS / designer px (see `branding/tokens.json`).
- **Motion:** Avoid seizure-risk flashes; prefer gentle transitions.
- **Audio:** Assume environments may be loud; do not rely on audio alone for learning goals. Provide on-screen text for key facts.
- **Touch (if used):** Large hit targets; obvious home/back affordance; idle timeout back to attract loop.
