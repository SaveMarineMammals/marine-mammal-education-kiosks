# Visitor-facing copy for Asian Small-Clawed Otters

Audience: general public, optimized for ~8-year-old reading level.
Voice: scannable bullets, plain text only for CMS (no Markdown, no emoji).
Layout: Glance & Match (Zone A static · Zone B art+copy · Zone C ticker).

## Zone A — Hero identification (static)

Asian Small-Clawed Otter  
Aonyx cinereus

| Metric | Value |
| --- | --- |
| Quick ID | Smallest otter · soft paws · wetland explorer |
| Max size | About 3 feet long (with tail) |
| Weight | About 11 pounds |
| Status | Vulnerable |

## Zone B — Paging insight cards (6s each · 24s loop)

Art sits in the upper band; copy sits in the lower band (never over the graphic).

### Card 1 — Diet

- Hunt crabs, snails, and shrimp
- Feel for food with soft paws
- Love shallow wetlands and rice fields

### Card 2 — Threat

- Wetlands shrink when people fill them
- Illegal pet trade takes wild otters
- Dirty water poisons their crab meals

### Card 3 — Hidden Fact

- Smallest otter species on Earth
- Live in family groups up to 15
- Use 12 or more calls to stay together

### Card 4 — How to Help

- Never keep wild otters as pets
- Keep trash and plastic out of water
- Share what you learned today
